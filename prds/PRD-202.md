## Abordare Tehnică

**Sursa date:** CME FedWatch Historical CSV export (manual, săptămânal)
- URL: https://www.cmegroup.com/markets/interest-rates/cme-fedwatch-tool.html → Historical → Downloads
- Naming convention: `FedMeetingHistory_YYYYMMDD.csv` (strict)
- Location: `data/market_pricing/fedwatch_snapshots/`

**Librării:** `pandas`, `numpy`, `scipy` (KL divergence), `pyarrow` (Parquet), `pydantic`, `fredapi` (for DFEDTARU/DFEDTARL current Fed Funds target)

**Pattern arhitectural:** thin loader over pre-computed institutional data, consistent cu D20 (Cleveland Fed Beige Book) și D23 (PRD-202 FedWatch manual).

**Surprise methods (toate implementate simultan, selecție empirică în PRD-300):**
1. `binary` — naive baseline (nlp_score − prob_hike), dimensionless, control group only
2. `expected_change` — Gürkaynak-Sack-Swanson (2005) industry standard, în basis points
3. `kl_divergence` — Kullback-Leibler distribution-level, în nats, always ≥ 0

**Default calibration:** 25bps per unit NLP hawkish (= one standard FOMC move size). OLS recalibration deferred la PRD-300 pe 80+ historical FOMC events.

## Empirical Findings

- CME publishes ~12 months forward (not 15 meetings as initially assumed)
- Drop zero probabilities saves 93% storage (~9220 non-zero vs ~143k raw per snapshot)
- ~90% of (observation_date, meeting_date) pairs sum to ≥0.95 probability mass (validates 63-buckets-per-block parser)

### Smoke test end-to-end (2026-04-15)

- 9220 rows loaded (1 snapshot, 2025-04-14 → 2026-04-13 range)
- Toate 3 metode surprise returnează magnitudini sensibile:
  - `binary`: 0.4897 (NLP 0.5 − prob_hike ~0.01)
  - `expected_change`: +12.2414 bps (consistent cu D25 calibration: 0.5 × 25bps ≈ 12.5bps − market expected ~0.25bps)
  - `kl_divergence`: 1.5977 nats (moderate divergence între NLP Gaussian synthetic și distribuția market)
