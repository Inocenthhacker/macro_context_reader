Boston Fed replicated 2025 → metodologie validată instituțional.

Refresh 6-8 săptămâni (acceptabil pentru orizont macro regime) — D20 rationale complet.

## Decizii legate

- **D20** — Cleveland Fed ICPSR indices > custom FinBERT pipeline. 8 iterations scraper (FIX1-FIX8) showed 11% publication loss; Cleveland Fed provides institutionally-validated scores; Boston Fed replicated 2025; 6-8 week refresh acceptable for macro regime horizon.
- **D21** — `national_consensus_divergence` as independent signal feature. Cleveland Fed (Filippou et al. 2024) documented systematic Fed narrative optimism vs district reality post-2020.
- **D22** — Economic sentiment weight in PRD-300 composite: ~10-15%. Zavodny & Ginther (2005) + Rosa (2013): Beige Book FX impact empirically weak vs real_rate_diff, FOMC surprise, COT positioning; filter/context layer, not primary driver.

## Technical Debt

- ICPSR automation (login + download) deferred — marginal value. Refresh manual săptămânal/lunar tolerabil.
- Custom FinBERT pipeline eliminat; dacă Cleveland Fed întrerupe publicarea, re-evaluare necesară.

## Surse academice

- Filippou, Garciga, Mitchell, Nguyen (Cleveland Fed 2024) — "Sentiment Indices from the Beige Book"
- Zavodny & Ginther (Southern Economic Journal 2005) — Beige Book moves bonds/equities marginally, weak FX impact
- Rosa (NY Fed 2013) — FOMC Statement/Minutes triple EUR/USD volatility intraday; Beige Book not tradable on FX
- openICPSR project 205881 — dataset DOI
